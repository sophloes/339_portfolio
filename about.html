About

I'm Sophie.

Photographer & Experience Designer & Adventure Seeker

London, England

June 2019


I’m a rising junior pursuing a Bachelor of Science in User Experience Design from the University of Michigan School of Information with a minor in History.

Photography has been a passion of mine for many years now. I am particularly interested in nature and travel photography. My design philosophy closely resembles my life philosophy: fueled by high levels of energy, I am constantly seeking play, adventure, and fulfillment.

In my free time, you can find me in the dance studio, watching British history documentaries, discovering the latest fashion trends, and planning my next travel adventure.
